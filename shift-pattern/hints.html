Shift Pattern - Hints

1:
Each answer begins with a different letter of the alphabet.
2:
Each answer has another property which, overall, appears once for each letter of the alphabet.
3:
"+13" might normally indicate a Caesar shift, but don't apply it to the normal alphabetical ordering.
4:
Associate each answer's starting letter with the other letter given by the property mentioned above. This gives rise to a cyclic ordering of the 26 letters in the alphabet to which the "+13" shift can be applied.
